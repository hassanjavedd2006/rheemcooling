OUR COMPANY

Rheem Cooling is a specialist Air Conditioner retail store. Founded in 1998, Rheem Cooling brings under one roof, multi-brand air conditioning products. We have over 20 brands in more than 40 stores across. Rheem Cooling brings alive the promise of a ‘Brighter Every Day’ with a world-class ambience to shop both in-store and online, while also creating a seamless omnichannel shopping experience that lets you enjoy the best of both, online and offline worlds.
