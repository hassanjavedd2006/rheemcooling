We sell both residential and commercial air conditioners from a variety of top brands including LG, SAMSUNG, Mitsubishi and TOSHIBA. Our selection includes split ac, window ac, cassette ac.

A split air conditioner is a type of system that uses two separate components: an indoor unit and an outdoor unit. The indoor unit is mounted inside the room, while the outdoor unit is installed outside. A window air conditioner, on the other hand, is a single, self-contained unit that is installed in a window or wall opening.

Ductless mini-split air conditioners offer a number of advantages over traditional systems, including energy efficiency, quiet operation, and flexible installation. They also allow you to set different temperatures in different areas of the home, which can save money on cooling costs.

Cassette air conditioners offer a number of advantages over traditional systems, including energy efficiency, whisper-quiet operation, and flexible installation. They provide a sleek, attractive look and can be installed in ceiling or wall cavities, reducing the amount of space taken up by the unit. They are also great for cooling large areas, as they have a wide air flow range.

The size of the air conditioner you need depends on the size of the room or area you want to cool. The larger the room or area, the more powerful the air conditioner should be. We recommend measuring the square footage of the space and then referring to the manufacturer’s size guide for the specific model you’re considering.
